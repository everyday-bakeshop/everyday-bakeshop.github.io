About Everday

I started baking with mochi (rich flour) due to my child’s health, when we discovered he had a sensitivity to wheat, eggs, and dairy. Even though he’s much better now, it changed the way I use ingredients and elevated my understanding of food’s impact on our bodies.

So in 2022 — inspired by my family, culture, and travels — I decided to dive 100% into Everyday Bakeshop in hopes of being able to share my creations with others.

With love,

Susan, Owner & Founder
