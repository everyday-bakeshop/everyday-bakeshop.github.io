Our mochi desserts are naturally gluten-friendly. They contain rice flour, coconut, eggs, sugar, and dairy. All desserts are baked in a facility that may come in contact with wheat, peanuts, soy, and tree nuts.

About Everday

I started baking with mochi (rich flour) due to my child’s health, when we discovered he had a sensitivity to wheat, eggs, and dairy. It changed the way I use ingredients, and elevated my understanding of food’s impact on our bodies.

So in 2022 — inspired by my family, culture, and travels — I decided to dive 100% into Everyday Bakeshop in hopes of being able to share my creations with others.

With love,

Susan, Owner & Founder
